About me

My name is Felix.
I’m a multimediadesign student at UCL.
I’m also a music producer, so when I’m not studying, that’s where I spend my energy.

With the knowledge and experience I’ve gained from the music industry, I know that sound creators want to focus on their passion and don’t always have time to learn new skills outside their field. That’s why I’ve decided to dedicate this site to you.

My goal is to supply music producers, sound designers, and sound creatives in general with graphics and visuals.

Whether it’s for digital products, releases, or anything else you might need. Focus on how your craft sounds, and let me worry about how it looks.
